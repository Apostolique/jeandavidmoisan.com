My favorite salad dressing recipe.

## Ingredients

* Canola oil, 1 Cup
* Vinegar, 1/3 Cup
* Sugar, 1 tbsp
* Salt, 1 1/2 tsp
* Paprika, 1/2 tsp
* Dry mustard, 1/2 tsp
* Garlic salt, 1/3 tsp
* Cayenne pepper, 1/3 tsp

## Preparation

Put all the ingredients in a bottle and mix.
